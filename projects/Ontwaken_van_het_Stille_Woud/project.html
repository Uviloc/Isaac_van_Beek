Writing a Book

During my 3rd year in Creative Media & Game Technologies I had to choose a minor.
The one I chose was Writing your Own Classic, a minor that would have you write a fiction book in just under half a years' time.

I always wanted to write a story and this felt like the perfect opportunity to do so.
My time there was absolutely amazing and I was able to learn a lot about the subject but also about myself and my future goals.
The book I wrote was a horror mystery novel about a 15-year-old boy getting lost in the woods where monsters live and thrive.

I simply posted just one chapter here to garner some interest, if you feel like you want to read the entire book, please contact me (do note this book is in Dutch)
